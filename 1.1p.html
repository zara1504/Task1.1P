Snoopy is an animated dog created by Charles M.Schulz. Snoopy has many friends including Woodstock his yellow fluffy bird, Charlie Brown his owner and many more. Snoopy the black and white beagle made his first appearence to the world in a comic strip on October 4th 1950, and has been seen across the world in different movies, shows and comic strips with his friends the peanut group. Snoopy the small cute puppy has a goofy and curious personality. He has made many people across the world happy and filled them with laughter since his first appearence.
